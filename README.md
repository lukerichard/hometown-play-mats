# Hometown Play Mats

A custom play-mat service that turns any neighborhood map into a premium, mouse-pad-style play surface for toy cars. Customers enter their address in a map UI, capture their block (or favorite area), and receive a durable, rollable mat with local streets, landmarks, and optional personalization.

## Vision
Give families a tactile, sentimental way to explore their hometowns: birthday gifts, moving presents, nostalgia pieces, and STEM toys rolled into one.

## Core Experience
1. **Map capture** – Google Maps-style UI with address search, draggable bounds, optional annotations.
2. **Design transformation** – stylized rendering pipeline (road simplification, color palette, labels, legend).
3. **Production** – print-on-demand on neoprene/mouse-pad substrate, stitched edges, multiple sizes.
4. **Delivery + upsells** – gift packaging, matching wall art, digital downloads.
